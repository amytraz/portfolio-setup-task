## Post 1: Content Stress Test
- Date: March 12, 2026  
- Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615_thats-the-thing-about-seo-in-most-cases-activity-7438068840657350656-wgVQ  

- Summary:  
Analyzes a large-scale experiment with 2,000 AI-generated articles published over a short period. While many pages were initially indexed, rankings declined over time, highlighting limitations of high-volume AI content.

- Key Insights:
  - High-volume AI content may gain short-term visibility but lacks long-term stability  
  - Google’s systems evaluate deeper quality signals beyond indexing  
  - E-E-A-T is critical for sustaining rankings in AI-driven search environments
