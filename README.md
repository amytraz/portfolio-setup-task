# AI-Powered SEO Content Production Research

## Topic
AI-powered SEO content production.

This repository collects practitioner content on how SEO and content teams are adapting to AI-driven search, answer engines, generative search results, and scalable AI-assisted content workflows.

## What I Collected
- 10 expert source profiles in `research/sources.md`
- 32 LinkedIn post analyses in `research/linkedin-posts/`
- 8 YouTube video research notes in `research/youtube-transcripts/`
- Collection methodology notes in `research/other/`

## Collection Method
LinkedIn posts were manually collected from public post URLs and summarized into structured notes. YouTube captions were collected with `yt-dlp` and distilled into research notes by video. Full transcripts are intentionally excluded so the repository stays focused and reviewable.

## Why These Experts
The experts were selected for direct involvement in building, advising, or operating SEO and content systems. The list favors practitioners with current, applied perspectives over generic commentary.

The selection balances:

- **Search quality and E-E-A-T:** Lily Ray, Aleyda Solis, Mike King
- **AI search / AEO / GEO:** Connor Kimball, Jason Barnard, Bernard Huang, Nathan Gotch
- **Semantic SEO and topical authority:** Koray Tugberk Gubur
- **Content distribution and B2B growth:** Ross Simmonds
- **Operational SEO and content systems:** Neil Patel

Together, these sources cover:

- What content to create
- How to structure content for AI systems
- How to improve machine readability and entity clarity
- How to distribute content across platforms
- How to measure visibility beyond traditional rankings
