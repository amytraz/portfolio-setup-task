# Research Methodology

## Assignment Fit
- Selected one topic: **AI-powered SEO content production**  
- Documented 10 experts in `research/sources.md`  
- Collected LinkedIn/blog post analyses for all experts  
- Collected YouTube research notes for 4 experts (8 videos total)  
- README explains scope, expert selection, and collection approach  

---

## Collection Method
- LinkedIn posts were manually collected from public URLs and normalized into structured research notes
- YouTube videos were reviewed using publicly available transcripts and AI-assisted tools, then distilled into concise research notes by video
- Content was filtered and synthesized to prioritize high-signal insights over raw data

Each entry follows a consistent structure:
- Summary  
- Key Insights  
- Practical Strategies  
- Tools / Workflows  

---

## Source Quality
- Experts are practitioners (founders, consultants, operators, or tool builders), not generic commentators  
- The set covers:
  - Technical SEO  
  - AEO / GEO (AI search optimization)  
  - Semantic SEO and topical authority  
  - Content optimization and search quality  
  - Content distribution and operational content systems  

The focus is on **applied, real-world strategies**, not theoretical discussion.
