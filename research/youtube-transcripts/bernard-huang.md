# Bernard Huang - YouTube Research Notes

## Video 1: Creating Content Google Cannot Ignore with Bernard Huang
- Date: November 9, 2022
- Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UaK-N7zGq70
- Channel: Jaryd Krause - Buying Online Businesses
- Duration: 55:48

- Summary:
Explains how to create high-performing SEO content by focusing on topical authority, search intent, and entity coverage. Emphasizes adapting to algorithm updates by prioritizing unique, high-quality content over large-scale production.

- Key Insights:
  - Topical authority built through entity coverage improves ranking stability
  - Search intent is more important than keyword targeting alone
  - Low-quality and repetitive content is penalized in modern search
  - Backlinks remain important but quality outweighs quantity
  - Engagement signals are becoming increasingly important
  - AI tools optimize for relevance rather than just scale

- Practical Strategies:
  - Break large guides into focused, standalone content pieces
  - Target high-intent and underserved topics
  - A/B test headlines and content for performance
  - Audit content for redundancy before publishing

- Tools / Workflows:
  - Clearscope for content optimization and entity analysis
  - Content auditing frameworks for identifying gaps

## Video 2: How to Build Systems for Content & SEO (+ Advanced Poker Tips) w/ Bernard Huang
- Date: April 8, 2024
- Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=33OLfnufbXg
- Channel: Omniscient
- Duration: 1:22:20

- Summary:
Discusses building scalable content systems for modern SEO, highlighting the importance of strategy, niche focus, and AI-assisted workflows. Emphasizes structured systems over isolated tactics.

- Key Insights:
  - Content strategy is more important than individual SEO tactics
  - AI helps scale content but requires filtering through engagement signals
  - Smaller sites can outperform larger ones with focused, high-quality content
  - Combining content and link building improves performance
  - AI search rewards depth and expertise over broad coverage

- Practical Strategies:
  - Build structured content systems around focused topics
  - Prioritize niche authority instead of broad content
  - Invest in user experience and design for engagement
  - Continuously update content for accuracy and relevance

- Tools / Workflows:
  - Clearscope for content scoring and optimization
  - A/B testing frameworks for improving engagement
